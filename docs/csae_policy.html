Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Effective Date: May 2025

Social Connect Hub is strongly committed to protecting children from sexual exploitation and abuse. We maintain zero tolerance for any content, behavior, or communication that promotes or facilitates child sexual abuse.

1. Prohibited Content

  • Explicit or suggestive content involving minors
  • Images, messages, or discussions promoting child sexual exploitation
  • Grooming behavior or solicitation of minors
  • Links or references to CSAE material

2. Detection & Moderation

We utilize Firebase and automated systems to monitor content. Users can also report any suspicious activity. Reported accounts are reviewed and may be suspended or permanently banned if found in violation.

3. User Reporting & Action

  • Users can report abuse via in-app tools
  • Violations are escalated to moderators and, if needed, legal authorities
  • Content is removed and offending accounts are terminated

4. Legal Compliance

We cooperate with law enforcement and comply with legal requirements in all jurisdictions where the app is available.

5. Commitment to Safety

Our team continuously reviews and updates safety protocols. We are committed to providing a secure, respectful environment for all users.
